About Fleet Parlour

Excellence, integrity, and dedication to craft.

Our Story

Fleet Parlour was founded on the belief that well-maintained vehicles represent the professionalism of their owners and leave lasting impressions on the road.

We specialise in professional metal polishing for trucks and heavy vehicles-restoring bull bars, fuel tanks, and rims to a high-gloss mirror finish with precision and care.
